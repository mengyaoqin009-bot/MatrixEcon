## 1. AUTODESK, INC. (GICS 45: Information Technology industry)

> **中文：** 1. AUTODESK, INC.（GICS 45：信息技术行业）

At its 2012 annual meeting, Autodesk Inc. received 54 percent shareholder support in its advisory vote for its executives’ compensation packages (the “say on pay” vote), which ranked that year in the bottom five percent of say on pay vote support in the Russell 3000. In the same year, Autodesk Inc. had also received an “against” vote recommendation by ISS for its say on pay vote.

> **中文：** 在 2012 年年度股东大会上，Autodesk Inc. 关于高管薪酬方案的咨询性投票，即“薪酬话语权”投票，仅获得 54% 的股东支持，在当年 Russell 3000 公司薪酬话语权投票支持率中位列最低的 5%。同年，ISS 还对 Autodesk Inc. 的薪酬话语权投票提出了“反对”建议。

In its research report, ISS singled out two areas of concerns about compensation: pay for performance alignment and peer group benchmarking policies. On the latter concern, ISS specifically criticized that Autodesk Inc.’s compensation peers did not fit with ISS’s relative-size criteria and compared Autodesk’s CEO pay to the median of its own peer group:

> **中文：** ISS 在研究报告中特别指出了两个薪酬方面的关切领域：薪酬与业绩的一致性，以及同业组基准比较政策。对于后一个问题，ISS 特别批评 Autodesk Inc. 的薪酬同业公司不符合 ISS 的相对规模标准，并将 Autodesk 的 CEO 薪酬与其自行选定的同业组中位数进行了比较：

“The company’s fiscal 2012 pay review and determination group comprises mainly of companies that are larger in terms of revenues. Moreover, several peers are more than two times larger in terms of revenue, and fiscal 2012 CEO pay was 2.18 times ISS’s peer group median. Making pay determinations based on companies that are primarily larger in terms of revenue may escalate pay irrespective of company performance.” (ISS research report on Autodesk, 18 May 2012, p.13)

> **中文：** “该公司 2012 财政年度的薪酬审查与确定组主要由收入规模更大的公司组成。此外，若干同业公司的收入规模超过该公司的两倍，而 2012 财政年度的 CEO 薪酬是 ISS 同业组中位数的 2.18 倍。主要依据收入规模更大的公司确定薪酬，可能会在不考虑公司业绩的情况下推高薪酬。”（ISS 关于 Autodesk 的研究报告，2012 年 5 月 18 日，第 13 页）

> **中文：** 在为 2014 年 1 月特别股东大会提交的委托书文件中，Autodesk Inc. 总结了其为回应股东在此前薪酬话语权投票中表达的不满而采取的措施：

“Continuing with its focus on instituting best practices for executive compensation, the Committee took a number of actions during fiscal 2012 aimed at evolving and improving Autodesk’s executive compensation programs. > **中文：** “委员会继续致力于建立高管薪酬最佳实践，并在 2012 财政年度采取了多项旨在发展和改进 Autodesk 高管薪酬计划的措施。这些措施包括：i）设计绩效股票单位计划；ii）调整 Autodesk 的薪酬同业组，使其与财务规模和业绩更接近 Autodesk 的公司保持一致；以及 iii）强制所有高管持有公司股票。”（Autodesk DEF14A，2013 年 12 月 3 日，第 18 页）

> **中文：** Autodesk 进一步表示，在 2013 财政年度再次经历令人失望的薪酬话语权投票、支持率仅为 64.7% 后，公司管理层联系了其最大股东。这些股东合计持有超过 60% 的流通股，公司希望借此了解他们对 Autodesk 高管薪酬政策的看法和关切。作为回应，薪酬委员会决定采取的措施包括：
