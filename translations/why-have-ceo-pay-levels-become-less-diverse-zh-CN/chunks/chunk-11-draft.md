### Table 7: ISS and peer group changes

> **中文：** 表 7：ISS 与同业组变动

This table examines firms’ decisions to drop peers from (add peers to) their compensation peer groups in t+1 in response to changes in ISS recommended peers for the firm in year t.

> **中文：** 本表考察公司针对 ISS 在年份 t 为其推荐的同业公司发生的变化，于 t+1 年从薪酬同业组中剔除同业公司（或向其中新增同业公司）的决策。

The dependent variable Dropped peer in t+1 (Added peer in t+1) in models 1-4 (models 5-8) is an indicator variable equal to one if a peer is dropped by a firm from (is newly added by a firm to) its compensation peer group in year t+1, else 0.

> **中文：** 模型 1-4（模型 5-8）的因变量 Dropped peer in t+1（Added peer in t+1）为指标变量；若公司在 t+1 年将某家同业公司从其薪酬同业组中剔除（新纳入其薪酬同业组），则取值为 1，否则为 0。

Just dropped below ISS cutoff is an indicator variable that is equal to one if a firm’s potential peer was just included in its ISS recommended peer group in year t−1 (i.e., relative rank between -4 and 0) but was just excluded from its ISS recommended peer group in year t (i.e., relative rank between 1 and 5).

> **中文：** Just dropped below ISS cutoff 为指标变量；若公司的某家潜在同业公司在 t−1 年刚好被纳入 ISS 推荐同业组（即相对排名介于 -4 和 0 之间），但在年份 t 刚好被排除在 ISS 推荐同业组之外（即相对排名介于 1 和 5 之间），则取值为 1。

Just rose above ISS cutoff is an indicator variable that is equal to one if a firm’s potential peer was just excluded from its ISS recommended peer group in year t−1 (i.e., relative rank between 1 and 5) but was just included in its ISS recommended peer group in year t (i.e., relative rank between -4 and 0).

> **中文：** Just rose above ISS cutoff 为指标变量；若公司的某家潜在同业公司在 t−1 年刚好被排除在 ISS 推荐同业组之外（即相对排名介于 1 和 5 之间），但在年份 t 刚好被纳入 ISS 推荐同业组（即相对排名介于 -4 和 0 之间），则取值为 1。

Control firms in models 4 and 8 are potential peers with relative ranks between -4 and 20 in years t and t+1 that did not switch into or out of ISS’s recommended peer group.

> **中文：** 模型 4 和模型 8 中的控制公司，是在年份 t 和 t+1 的相对排名均介于 -4 和 20 之间、且未进入或退出 ISS 推荐同业组的潜在同业公司。
