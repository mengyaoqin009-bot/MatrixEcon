> **中文：** 薪酬离散度（经济总体、行业或行业-规模层面）

Standard deviation of Pay where Pay is defined as CEO pay (TDC1 in Execucomp) scaled by its median value in that year and winsorized at the 1st and 99th percentiles. When computed at the industry-level, pay dispersion is computed across firms within the same GICS6-industry in a given year. Pay dispersion (abnormal)

> **中文：** 薪酬离散度（异常）

Standard deviation of Abnormal pay. Abnormal pay is computed as the residual from the following cross-sectional regression (estimated for each year separately):

CEO pay<sub>i,t</sub> = α<sub>t</sub> + β<sub>t</sub> ∗ firm characteristics<sub>i,t−1</sub> + γ<sub>s,t</sub> + ϵ<sub>i,t</sub>

where CEO pay is the unscaled reported pay, winsorized at the 1st and 99th percentiles. The suffixes i, t, s refer to firm i, year t and industry-size quintile s. The vector of firm characteristics includes firm size, stock return, sales growth, Tobin’s Q, and idiosyncratic return volatility. The residuals from the annual regressions are then scaled by the median CEO pay in the year. The dispersion is computed as the standard deviation of the scaled residuals (depending on the level of aggregation - i.e., either across all firms in the economy, across all firms in an industry-size group, or across all compensation peers of a firm).

> **中文：** Abnormal pay 的标准差。Abnormal pay 按照以下横截面回归的残差计算（每年分别估计）：
>
> CEO pay<sub>i,t</sub> = α<sub>t</sub> + β<sub>t</sub> ∗ firm characteristics<sub>i,t−1</sub> + γ<sub>s,t</sub> + ϵ<sub>i,t</sub>
>
> 其中，CEO pay 是未经缩放的报告薪酬，并在第 1 和第 99 百分位数处进行缩尾处理。下标 i、t、s 分别表示公司 i、年份 t 和行业-规模五分位数组 s。公司特征向量包括公司规模、股票收益率、销售增长率、Tobin’s Q 和特质收益波动率。随后，将年度回归所得残差除以该年度 CEO 薪酬的中位数。离散度计算为缩放后残差的标准差，具体取决于汇总层面，即基于经济总体中的全部公司、某个行业-规模组中的全部公司，或者一家公司的全部薪酬同业公司计算。
