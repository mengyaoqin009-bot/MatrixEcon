Figure IA.5: Realized pay dispersion

> **中文：** 图 IA.5：已实现薪酬的离散度

This figure shows dispersion in realized CEO pay between 2006 and 2023 for Execucomp firms (a lack of detailed pay data prevents its computation pre-2006). Realized pay v1 is defined as total CEO pay reported in the DEF14 filing to the SEC, except that grant date fair values of stock and option awards are replaced with the value of stocks vested during the year and the value realized from options exercised during the year (=Execucomp item TOTAL ALT2), winsorized at the 5th and 95th percentiles. Realized pay v2 follows the definition of the CAP2 measure (CAP=“Compensation Actually Paid”) by Carter and Pawliczek (2023). This measure makes the following changes to the reported pay: (i) recalculates the value of stock and option grants made at the start of the year by multiplying their value with the percent of stock price appreciation that occurred during the year, (ii) adds the change in the value of outstanding unvested stock and options by multiplying the number of units times the stock price appreciation during the year, and (iii) adds the change in the value of shares vested during the year (i.e., value of vested shares minus number of shares vested times price at the beginning of the year). The measure is winsorized at the 5th and 95th percentiles. Finally, to compute the dispersion of both measures, we scale each measure by its median value in a given year and take the standard deviation of the scaled measure. For further details, please see the Variable appendix.

> **中文：** 本图展示了 Execucomp 公司在 2006—2023 年间已实现 CEO 薪酬的离散度（由于缺少详细的薪酬数据，无法计算 2006 年以前的该指标）。已实现薪酬 v1 定义为公司向 SEC 提交的 DEF14 文件中报告的 CEO 薪酬总额，但将股票和期权奖励的授予日公允价值替换为年内归属股票的价值以及年内行权期权的已实现价值（=Execucomp 项目 `TOTAL ALT2`），并在第 5 和第 95 百分位数处进行缩尾处理。已实现薪酬 v2 遵循 Carter and Pawliczek (2023) 对 CAP2 指标的定义，其中 CAP 表示“实际支付的薪酬”（Compensation Actually Paid）。该指标对报告薪酬作出以下调整：(i) 将年初授予的股票和期权的价值乘以年内股票价格升值百分比，重新计算其价值；(ii) 将尚未归属的流通在外股票和期权的单位数量乘以年内股票价格升值额，并加入其价值变动；(iii) 加入年内已归属股份的价值变动，即已归属股份的价值减去已归属股份数量与年初价格的乘积。该指标在第 5 和第 95 百分位数处进行缩尾处理。最后，在计算两个指标的离散度时，我们将每个指标除以其在相应年份的中位数，并计算缩放后指标的标准差。更多详细信息请参见变量附录。
